## **🚀 Handling CEO Objections**

### **"How do we know it works?"**
**Response:** *"95% accuracy in back-testing on historical data where we know the actual outcomes. Plus 30-day manual validation period."*

### **"What about false alarms?"**
**Response:** *"Better to investigate 10 false alarms than miss one $50,000 failure. We validate every alert initially."*

### **"Can we afford this right now?"**
**Response:** *"We can't afford NOT to do this. One prevented emergency repair pays for the entire system."*

### **"What if staff resist the change?"**
**Response:** *"We position this as enhancing their expertise, not replacing it. Early wins build confidence quickly."*

### **"How long until we see results?"**
**Response:** *"First predictions within days, first prevented failure within 30 days, full ROI within 6 months."*
