Quick FAQs

Portable Services

  • How can Scholars 360 help me study abroad?

    We guide you from university selection to admission, visa, and post-arrival support.

  • Which countries can I apply to?

    USA, UK, Canada, Australia, New Zealand, Germany, Ireland & more.

  • Do I need work experience for a Master’s?

    Not always — but top MBAs prefer 2–3 years of experience.

  • When should I start preparing?

    12–18 months before intake for smooth applications.

  • Can I work part-time while studying?

    Yes, most countries allow 20 hours/week during study.

  • Do you assist with loans and scholarships?

    Yes, we provide guidance for both financial aid and loans.

  • What English tests are accepted?

    IELTS, TOEFL, PTE, and Duolingo (varies by country).

  • Do you help with SOPs & LORs?

    Yes, we offer expert support for SOPs, essays, and LORs.

  • How long does visa processing take?

    Usually 3–8 weeks, depending on the country.

  • What support do I get after arrival?

    Help with housing, SIM, bank account & part-time jobs.
